Digital Forensics Challenge

Put your cybersecurity investigation skills to the test. Analyze digital evidence to solve the case and identify the culprit behind a data breach.

Mission Brief

A financial technology company has experienced a data breach. Customer financial data was exfiltrated from their systems. Your task is to analyze the digital evidence collected and determine:

  • Who was responsible for the breach
  • How they gained access to the system
  • What specific data was compromised
  • When the breach occurred

Evidence Files

  • Security Camera Image
  • System Log Excerpt
  • Command History
  • Suspicious Email
  • Metadata Analysis

Case Solution

Look carefully at all evidence files. Pay special attention to:

  • The timeline across different evidence files
  • IP addresses and their connections
  • The phishing email domain vs. the legitimate company domain
  • Physical access evidence combined with digital evidence
